1. Expressive Typography


Made during Year 1
Used Adobe Illustrator


Expressive Typography design,
made to represent who I was in
a dark time. The bright colours
represent escapism, with the
bleeding ink to represent
feeling bogged down and trapped,
with the red cracks being
negative voices. It's a rather
personal piece of work.








2. Alphabet Design

Made during Year 1
Used Adobe Illustrator

Alphabet design. I chose for it to based
on creatures and monsters. Out of the three
colour schemes I came up with, this is
my favorite due to the eerie vibe it
gave off which fit the theme well.









3. Mock Travel Magazine

Made during year one
Used Adobe Photoshop

Advertising Okinawa.
I made sure to use the photos provided
without feeling too stiff, with colours

simple text fonts to feel less overwhelming
but to ensure the descriptions are readable.
